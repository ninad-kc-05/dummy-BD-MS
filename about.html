About LifeFlow

LifeFlow is a focused blood donation management platform that brings donors, hospitals, and patients onto a single, coordinated system.

Our goal is to make blood availability more predictable and transparent by simplifying how requests are raised, matched, and fulfilled across different user roles.

Real-time donor–hospital coordination Role-based dashboards for admins, donors & receivers Transparent request tracking & notifications

User Roles

3

Admin, Donor, Receiver

Primary Objective

Ensure timely availability of blood by reducing shortages and improving coordination between donors and healthcare institutions.

LifeFlow is built as an academic project that showcases real-world system design, clean UI, and multi-role user flows inspired by production-grade platforms.
